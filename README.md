# Credit-Card-Financial-Dashboard

Project Overview:
This project analyzes credit card customer financial data to gain insights into spending behavior, credit utilization, payment patterns, and customer demographics. Using Power BI, an interactive dashboard is built to provide transparency into customer activity, identify credit risk, detect overspending, and support data-driven financial strategies.

Objectives:
Analyze customer spending distribution across categories
Compare actual credit usage against forecasted credit limits
Identify payment behavior patterns (on-time vs late payments)
Assess credit risk based on outstanding balances and payment trends
Support targeted marketing and risk mitigation strategies

Dataset:
The dataset contains credit card customer financial information, including:
Customer demographics (age, gender, income)
Credit limits and credit utilization
Spending by category (travel, entertainment, utilities, etc.)
Payment history and payment status
Outstanding balances
Data is sourced from CSV / Excel files and imported into Power BI.

Tools & Technologies:
Power BI
Power Query
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
Excel / CSV

Project Workflow
1. Data Preparation
2. Data Cleaning & Standardization
3. Data Structuring

Dashboard Features:
Spending distribution by category
Credit utilization vs credit limit comparison
Payment behavior analysis (on-time vs late)
Credit risk indicators based on outstanding balance trends
Interactive slicers for demographics and customer segments

Key Insights:
Identified high-spending categories and overspending customers
Highlighted customers with high credit utilization and payment delays
Revealed patterns linking demographics with credit risk
Enabled proactive financial and risk management decisions

Business Value:
Supports credit risk assessment and monitoring
Enables targeted financial and marketing strategies
Improves visibility into customer payment behavior
Helps optimize credit limit and policy decisions
